Squidward Tentacles

Everything about Squidward. The page, Just for YOU!

Squidward Quincy Tentacles is a turquoise colored cephalopod. His species is an octopus despite his name containing the word "Squid" and having 6 legs instead of 8. Squidward lives in a house shaped like an Easter Island Head (Moai Head)

Squidward's Life

Squidward childhood was very difficult. He grew up in Biking Bottom, and while in school (which remains unknown) he was rejected by everyone, therefore had no friends. He sat near the garbage can to eat his lunch at school.

When older, he started working for Eugene Krabs, in the Krusty Krab. His life was fine until, Spongebob Squarepants came along. His life became boring and lame with his "annoying neighbours."
